HR Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)

📊 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive HR Analytics Dashboard built using Power BI to analyze employee attrition, workforce demographics, and salary distribution.
The dashboard helps organizations understand key factors affecting employee turnover and supports data-driven HR decisions.

🎯 Objectives

- Analyze overall employee attrition
- Identify high-risk age groups and job roles
- Understand salary impact on attrition
- Explore employee distribution by education and department

📈 Key Metrics

- Total Employees: 1470
- Attrition Count: 238
- Attrition Rate: 16.2%
- Average Age: 37
- Average Salary: 6.5K
- Average Years at Company: 7.0

📊 Dashboard Insights

👥 Attrition by Age

- Highest attrition observed in 26–35 age group (116 employees)
- Followed by 18–25 and 36–45 age groups

💰 Attrition by Salary

- Majority attrition in salary below 5K (163 employees)
- Very low attrition in high salary groups

🎓 Attrition by Education

- Life Sciences: 37% (highest)
- Medical: 26%
- Marketing: 15%
- Technical: 13%
- Others: Remaining share

🧑‍💼 Attrition by Job Role

- Laboratory Technician: 62 (highest)
- Sales Executive: 58
- Research Scientist: 47
- Sales Representative: 33

⏳ Attrition by Years at Company

- Highest attrition occurs in early years (0–2 years)
- Gradually decreases as experience increases

⚧ Attrition by Gender

- Male Employees: 889
- Female Employees: 591
